## What's Included in the Package

### 1. ATF Oil Replacement

The most critical part of gearbox maintenance. We drain your old, degraded transmission fluid and replace it with fresh ATF matched to your vehicle's specification.

Old ATF loses its hydraulic properties, lubricating ability, and heat resistance over time. If your fluid is dark, smells burnt, or has never been changed past 60,000 km — your gearbox is running on borrowed time.

### 2. Auto Filter Change

The transmission filter catches metal shavings, clutch material debris, and other contaminants before they circulate through your gearbox. A clogged filter restricts fluid flow, causing:

- Delayed gear engagement
- Overheating
- Increased wear on internal components

We replace the filter during every service to ensure clean fluid flow throughout the system.

### 3. FREE OBD2 Diagnostic Device

This is what makes the package different. Every customer receives a **Bluetooth OBD2 scanner** — a plug-and-play device that connects to your phone and lets you:

- Read engine and transmission error codes
- Monitor real-time data (RPM, temperature, voltage)
- Catch problems early before they become expensive repairs
- Clear warning lights after confirmed fixes

Most workshops charge RM 50–80 for the device alone. We include it free because we believe every driver should have visibility into their car's health — not just when something goes wrong.

## Who Should Get This Package?

This package is ideal if you fall into any of these categories:

- **Your ATF has never been changed** — or you're not sure when it was last done
- **Your car is past 60,000 km** — the common threshold for first ATF service
- **You bought a used car** — and have no service history for the gearbox
- **You're experiencing early symptoms** — slight shudder, delayed engagement, or rough shifting
- **You want preventive maintenance** — before problems start, not after

If your gearbox already has severe symptoms (loud grinding, complete slipping, no drive), you may need a diagnosis or overhaul instead. We'll advise you honestly during inspection.
