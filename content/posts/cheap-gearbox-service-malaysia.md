## What Does Gearbox Service Actually Cost?

| Component | Estimated Price |
|---|---|

## Why "Too Cheap" Is Dangerous

Some workshops offer gearbox service for as low as RM 80–100. This usually means:

1. **Wrong oil specification** — Cheap oil can damage seals and clutch packs
2. **No filter replacement** — Saves cost but debris keeps circulating in the system
3. **Incomplete flush** — Only a portion of the oil is changed, not the full volume

Don't be fooled by low prices without knowing what you're actually getting.
