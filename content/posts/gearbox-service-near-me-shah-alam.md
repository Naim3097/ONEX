If you're in Shah Alam, Klang, Subang Jaya, Petaling Jaya, or anywhere in the Klang Valley, here's what you need to know before choosing a workshop.

## Why You Need a Specialist, Not Just a Nearby Workshop

A general mechanic might offer to "look at your gearbox," but transmission work requires:

- **Specific diagnostic tools** — Generic scanners often can't read transmission-specific fault codes
- **Correct fluid specifications** — Using the wrong ATF or CVT fluid can cause immediate damage
- **Internal component knowledge** — Valve bodies, solenoids, clutch packs, and torque converters all require specialist understanding
- **Model-specific experience** — A Perodua Myvi CVT behaves very differently from a Toyota Vios automatic

## Red Flags When Choosing a Gearbox Workshop

Watch out for these warning signs:

1. **"We can fix any gearbox problem for RM 200"** — If it sounds too cheap, the service will match
2. **No diagnostic equipment visible** — A real transmission shop has OBD scanners, pressure testers, and test benches
3. **Reluctance to explain the problem** — A good specialist will show you the worn parts and explain what failed
4. **Pressure to decide immediately** — "If you don't fix it today, it'll cost double next week" is a manipulation tactic

## What a Quality Gearbox Service Looks Like

A proper service should include:

### For Regular Maintenance
- Drain old ATF/CVT fluid completely
- Replace transmission filter
- Refill with manufacturer-specified fluid
- Test drive to verify smooth operation
- Check for leaks

### For Diagnosis
- OBD2 scan for transmission fault codes
- Road test to replicate the reported symptoms
- Visual inspection of external components (mounts, linkages, seals)
- Fluid condition assessment
- Clear explanation of findings with repair options and pricing

### For Overhaul
- Complete disassembly of the transmission
- Inspection of all internal components
- Replacement of worn clutch packs, seals, gaskets, and bearings
- Torque converter inspection or replacement
- Reassembly and calibration
- Extended road testing
